Superadmin Impersonation Guide

Multi-Level User Access Demonstration

1. Overview

CoreHR provides a hierarchical impersonation feature that allows superadmins to log in as any user in the system without knowing their password. This is essential for customer support, debugging, and demonstrating the platform's multi-tenant capabilities.

Impersonation Hierarchy

Superadmin → HR Consultant → Company Admin → ESS Employee

Each level provides a progressively more restricted view of the platform, allowing you to demonstrate how different user types experience CoreHR.

4. Impersonation Process

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Login to Superadmin Portal

Navigate to https://corehr.africa/superadmin/login and enter your superadmin credentials.

2

Navigate to Consultants Page

Click on "HR Consultants" in the sidebar to view all registered consultants.

3

Expand Consultant Row

Click the arrow (▸) next to a consultant to expand and view their client companies.

4

Choose Impersonation Level

Option A: Click "Impersonate" button to login as the consultant.
Option B: Click "Impersonate Admin" on a company to login as company admin.
Option C: Expand a company, then click "ESS" on an employee to login as that employee.

5

View Impersonation Banner

A colored banner appears at the top showing who you're impersonating and who initiated the session.

6

End Impersonation

Click "End Session" in the banner to log out and close the impersonation window.

7. Security Notes

Audit Trail

All impersonation sessions are logged with timestamp, superadmin identity, target user, and session duration.

  • Impersonation tokens expire after 1 hour for security
  • The impersonation banner cannot be hidden or dismissed
  • All actions performed during impersonation are attributed to the impersonated user but logged with superadmin context
  • Impersonation sessions open in a new browser tab to preserve the superadmin session
  • "End Session" immediately invalidates the impersonation token

8. Demonstration Workflow

Recommended sequence for demonstrating CoreHR's multi-tenant architecture:

  1. Start as Consultant: Show the full HR platform with CRM, Business Development, and multi-company management
  2. Switch to Company Admin: Demonstrate how a client's HR team sees only their company's data (employees, payroll, compliance)
  3. Switch to ESS Employee: Show the employee self-service experience (payslips, leave requests, onboarding, training)
  4. Return to Superadmin: End all sessions and show the audit log of impersonation activities
