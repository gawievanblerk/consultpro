## Executive Summary

Management seeks Board approval for a **$7,500 investment** to develop and launch **ConsultPro**, a SaaS platform for HR consulting firms in the African market.

### Investment Highlights

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total Investment** | $7,500 |
| **Payback Period** | 8 months |
| **5-Year NPV** | $298,457 |
| **5-Year IRR** | 312% |
| **Year 5 ARR** | $450,000 |

### Strategic Rationale

1. **Market Gap**: No integrated CRM-ERP exists for HR consulting firms in Africa
2. **First-Mover**: Establish market leadership before competitors enter
3. **Leverage**: 60% codebase reuse from existing Rozitech products
4. **Anchor Customer**: TeamACE Nigeria as founding partner reduces market risk

## 7. Risk Assessment

### 7.1 Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **TeamACE doesn't refer customers** | Low | Medium | Contractual referral commitment |
| **Slow market adoption** | Medium | Medium | TeamACE validates product-market fit |
| **SeamlessHR enters space** | Low | High | Different market segment (consulting vs internal HR) |
| **Technical issues** | Low | Medium | 60% proven codebase reuse |
| **Payment collection** | Medium | Low | Partner with Paystack/Flutterwave |
| **Competition from global CRM** | Medium | Low | Africa-specific features, local pricing |

### 7.2 Downside Scenario
