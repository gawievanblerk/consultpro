## Notes

- CTC = Cost to Company (includes UIF, SDL, leave provisions)
- Actual take-home is ~75-80% of CTC after tax
- These rates are competitive for Johannesburg/Cape Town market
- Remote work may allow 10-15% lower rates from other regions
- Consider 13th cheque / performance bonuses not included above
- 50-60% code reuse could reduce hours by 15-20% in practice
- Conservative estimate; actual may be $480K-$520K with efficient reuse
- Doesn't include infrastructure costs (Render.com, etc.)
