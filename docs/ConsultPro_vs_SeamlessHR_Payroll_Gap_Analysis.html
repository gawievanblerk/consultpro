Priority Gap Analysis

High Priority

Critical payroll functionality for enterprise clients

1. Payroll Reports & Exports

  • Bank summary report for payment processing
  • CSV/Excel export for all reports
  • Cost to company and departmental reports
  • Variance reports between payroll runs

2. Bank Schedule Generation

  • Generate bank file for bulk salary transfers
  • Support multiple bank formats (CBN, NIBSS)
  • Payment reconciliation tracking

3. Multi-Step Approval Workflow

  • Configurable approval chain (HR → Finance → MD)
  • Email notifications at each step
  • Two-factor authentication for sensitive approvals

4. Email Payslips to Employees

  • Bulk send payslips after approval
  • PDF attachment with password protection
  • Delivery confirmation tracking

5. Year-to-Date (YTD) Tracking

  • YTD gross, tax, pension on payslips
  • YTD dashboard for employees
  • Annual tax summary for employees
Medium Priority

Enhanced functionality for operational efficiency

6. Pay Grades System

Standardized pay structures with automatic allowance/deduction assignment based on grade level

7. Overtime & Timesheet Integration

Track overtime hours and automatically include in payroll calculation

8. Pro-rata Calculations

Automatic proration for mid-month joiners/leavers and unpaid leave

9. Bulk Upload Functions

Excel/CSV upload for allowances, deductions, and payroll adjustments

10. Configurable Payslip Templates

Company-specific payslip layouts with selectable fields and branding

Lower Priority

Advanced features for comprehensive platform
