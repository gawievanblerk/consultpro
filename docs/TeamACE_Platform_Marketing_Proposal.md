## Transform Your HR Consulting Operations

ConsultPro is a purpose-built business platform designed exclusively for HR consulting and outsourcing firms. Unlike generic CRM or HR software, ConsultPro understands your unique business model - managing client relationships, deploying staff, tracking engagements, and ensuring you get paid on time.

## The Challenge

HR consulting firms face unique operational challenges that generic software cannot address:

| Challenge | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| **Scattered Client Data** | Client information spread across spreadsheets, emails, and paper files |
| **Lost Opportunities** | No systematic way to track leads and sales pipeline |
| **Staff Deployment Chaos** | Difficulty tracking which staff are deployed where |
| **Invoicing Delays** | Manual invoice creation leads to delayed payments |
| **No Visibility** | Leadership lacks real-time insight into business performance |

**The result?** Lost revenue, inefficient operations, and frustrated teams.

### 1. Client Relationship Management

**Know your clients inside and out.**

Build lasting client relationships with a complete 360-degree view of every client.

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Complete Client Profiles** | All client information in one place - company details, contracts, history |
| **Contact Management** | Track every key contact with roles, preferences, and communication history |
| **Engagement Tracking** | Monitor all projects and services per client |
| **Document Library** | Store and organize contracts, proposals, and reports |
| **Activity Timeline** | Never miss a follow-up with complete interaction history |
| **Client Portal** | Optional self-service access for your clients |

**Key Capabilities:**
- Quick client search and filtering
- Custom fields for your specific needs
- Bulk import from existing spreadsheets
- Client health scoring
- Automatic relationship reminders
